Edit Product Details: Admin users have the authority to edit existing product details, such as title, description, price, images, and specifications. This functionality allows admins to keep product information accurate and up-to-date, reflecting any changes in inventory or specifications.

Add Product: Admin users can add new products to the online store catalog. They can input product information, upload images, set pricing, specify categories and tags, and provide detailed descriptions. This feature enables the expansion of the product offerings and the introduction of new items to attract customers.

Delete Products: The admin also has the capability to delete products from the online store. This functionality allows the admin to remove products that are no longer available, out of stock, or no longer meet the store's criteria.

Customer Login: Customers can create an account or log in using their credentials. This allows them to access their personalized account information, including order history, saved addresses, and payment methods.

View Products: Once logged in, customers can browse through the online product catalog. Products are displayed with images, descriptions, prices, and other relevant details. Customers can filter and sort products based on various criteria, such as category, price range, brand, and popularity.

Buy Products: Customers can add desired products to their shopping cart and proceed to checkout. At checkout, they can review their selected items, choose a shipping method, and provide billing and shipping information. Secure payment options are available for completing the purchase.

View Customer Bill: After successfully placing an order, customers receive an order confirmation email with a summary of their purchase, including the list of items, total amount, shipping details, and estimated delivery date. Customers can also view their order history and print invoices from their account dashboard.
